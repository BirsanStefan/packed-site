Frequently asked questions

What's included in a packed holiday?

To quote the great Cilla Black, the choice... is yours! We can include flights, transfers, accommodation and any extras you might like to include.

For ski trips, we normally add equipment hire, lift passes and lessons as well.

We can also include events, activities, special requests, pretty much anything. We can also advise if you're not sure!

How does the app help?

Our app is for sorting out your group, which we know can be a struggle. The app simplifies it all - invite your mates and vote on where to go, dates and any other decisions you need to make.

Once you've got your plan, chat to us and we'll find you the best deals.

Do I pay extra to use packed rather than booking myself?

Not a penny more! We match the prices you'd find direct with hotels and villas, or on websites such as Booking.com and Expedia.

We receive a small fee from travel providers (smaller and fairer than these big guys!) for bringing lovely people like you to them.

Do I have to pay for the whole group myself?

Nope, everyone pays separately on the personalised booking page we set up for you.

How is my money protected?

We are a member of the Travel Trust Association and have an ATOL licence.

Am I required to pay for everything upfront?

Typically you pay a deposit to secure a booking but you may need to pay in full to get cheaper, non-refundable rates, or if you're departing soon.

Do I need insurance?

Yes, it really is essential to cover you for things like accidents, missed flights or travel disruptions. It's a condition of booking that all passengers must purchase their own travel insurance.

We recommend World Nomads, but wherever you purchase your insurance, please check the policy wording to make sure you have the cover you need
