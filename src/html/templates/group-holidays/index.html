Great group holidays

Nothing beats a big group holiday with your friends, family or favourite work colleagues.

Whether you're planning a rowdy stag do for your best mate, an epic corporate retreat for the office or you simply want some quality time away with your loved ones, there are so many reasons why group holidays are the way to go...

Group holidays are fun, sociable and memorable, and they often work out better for your bank balance, too. If you’re sharing the cost of accommodation, you’ll be able to go for something awesome - think a luxurious villa with an infinity pool in Spain or a cool ski chalet in the Alps. What’s more, once-in-a-lifetime activities like helicopter rides and private catamaran trips can be up for grabs at a fraction of the price if you’re dividing all the costs.

With packed, you can tailor your group trip to suit everyone and then split the costs between you - which just makes good sense.
Find group holidays challenging? Just book with packed

You might be sold on the idea of a big group holiday, but being nominated the designated travel planner can mean a lot of hassle. You need to work out everyone's availability, decide on a location and find suitable accommodation for the entire group. And that's all without the added stress of chasing everyone up for deposits...

Nothing screams awkward like chasing up your friend for the 7th time in a row and getting nothing but blue ticks back. Luckily, this is where packed can save the day (and your sanity)...

Our team are experts when it comes to coordinating epic group travel, and they're just a quick and convenient chat away. Between us, we've been on hundreds of group trips and we've helped to plan thousands, so we know what it takes to plan the getaway of the century. We take care of all the nitty gritty for you, including securing payments from everyone individually so you - as the group leader - are never out of pocket.

When all the essential details are sorted, we'll book everything on your behalf, whether that's flights and airport transfers, amazing accommodation or a kickass itinerary of activities. Need more info? Check out our how it works page.

Organising your friends shouldn't take 1,000 WhatsApps

One of the big challenges in getting your group on holiday is getting commitment. We launched the packed app to help you get your team together faster and with less stress.

It's like Doodle for group holidays, and lets you poll friends for the dates they're available, agree on location, and even lets you all vote on how much you're willing to spend (anonymous voting is an option).

You can also request travel information like full names, dates of birth and dietary requirements at the tap of a button. Real handy, especially for large groups.

So, what sort of group holiday do you fancy?

From stag and hen dos through to rowdy weekends away with your teammates, there are so many great group holidays we can help you plan out and pull off.

Whether it's your annual girls' trip, your nan's 90th or an epic ski trip designed to bring people together, we'll make sure it goes smoothly from start to finish. We're there every step of the way, so you can focus all your energy on more important things like putting together the perfect holiday wardrobe or making a list of all the bars and restaurants you want to visit.

Zen yoga retreat, thrilling ski getaway, lazy week in the Med - tailor your getaway to be as relaxed or as active as you like. No idea is too wild and if you've got any special requests, we'll do our best to get them sorted for you.
