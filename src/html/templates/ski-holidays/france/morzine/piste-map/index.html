Morzine makes up just one section of the huge Portes du Soleil region and it's an awesome resort for both unforgettable ski weekends or longer holidays.

Linked to the neighbouring resort of Les Gets, a ski pass for both areas gives you access to over 60 marked runs, with plenty of options for every ability. Staying for more than a couple of days? The Portes du Soleil pass and piste map are definitely for you, with access to a huge 650km of pistes!

While beginners are well-catered for with lots of nice nursery runs and breezy blues, those up for a challenge will find dozens of epic off-piste routes using the Morzine FATMAP. Keen to spend just as much time partying as you do on the slopes? Take a look at our resort map of Morzine to discover all the best spots for wining and dining.
