The double-edged sword

Rugby tour. Glorious, delicious tour. Anyone who’s been involved in a rugby tour or two can attest to the memories or lack there-of they create. Unfortunately, usually for one or two people, they are a nightmare to arrange. The key challenges are:
  • It’s time-consuming searching for options, working out logistics and sending emails to hotels & providers
  • Making payment and collecting reimbursements is awkward
  • Working out who’s available and gathering travel details can be a nuisance

Making things simple

We founded packed 2 years ago to solve these problems and make it easier for big groups to travel together.
  • You tell us what you’re looking for in terms of dates, destinations and a rough budget per person
  • We present relevant options on an easy-to-share link
  • Then we set up a dedicated booking page where everyone can log in and pay separately
We always price-match our packages so the trips never cost any more if booked through us vs any other route (we take our commission from the end providers such as the hotels, transfer companies etc, which is common practice in the travel industry) and we hopefully save someone hours of their time and alleviate some hassle.
Founded by rugby players, we know the value of a rugby tour. There are few better occasions and our service, designed to help make tours happen, is a lot better than our play ever was.

What we can do

Our trips are always completely tailored to the group's requirements and we arrange ski trips as well as summer tours. Packages can include flights, transfers, accommodation plus any activities the group wants to take part in.
Summer tours from Cornwall to Perpignan and beyond. Mid-season internationals. Winter breaks to the slopes, weekend or week long. You decide what you’re looking for and we make it happen. We can even arrange tour polos and the first night’s bar tab.
If this sounds like something you or your club could be interested in, please either drop us a line in WhatsApp, Messenger or good old reliable email using one of the links below. We’d love to help.

Our app helps you plan your tour

Our mobile app is a bit like doodle for group trips and helps you work out who’s available for a given date, how much everyone’s willing to spend and where everyone might like to go, as an alternative to manic WhatsApp groups or email chains. You can also gather everyone’s travel info and dietary requirements in one tap.
