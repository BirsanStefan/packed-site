Great group holidays

What’s more, once-in-a-lifetime activities like helicopter rides and private catamaran trips can be up for grabs at a fraction of the price if you’re dividing all the costs.

With packed, you can tailor your group trip to suit everyone and then split the costs between you - which just makes good sense.
