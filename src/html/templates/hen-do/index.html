Let’s get this hen party started!

So, you are here because you have a hen weekend to plan. That not so tiny task to treat the bride-to-be to the perfect send-off before the big day. Between dreaming up destinations, managing the “Mr and Mrs”, dealing on dates, agreeing on activities, and generally making the sure she has the absolute BEST TIME EVER, you might be forgiven for feeling a little less over the moon, and a little more under the bus. After all it’s a hell of a lot to handle.
Well listen up ladies because the beauty of our chat-based service is that we help with all this hen party planning. For some, L-plates and living it large in the legendary Marbs might be hen do heaven. For others, a secluded sun-soaked spa in Santorini is the destination of their dreams. But rowdy or relaxed, we understand it is personal preference, which is why we painstakingly pick packages bespoke to the bride-to-be - whilst ensuring you get the most bang for your buck.
To avoid any awkward payment arrangements, we set up separate payment links for every hen party goer - and even do the chasing for you. Leaving you to focus on more pressing problems - like what games will get the girls going and whether willy-shaped accessories are acceptable given the attendance of the bride's ancient aunt.
